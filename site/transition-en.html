GCTools

Enabling public servants, digitally

With GCTools, we made it easier to find the resources and people you need inside and outside the public service to connect, share ideas, and collaborate.

Consistent with Blueprint 2020, we envision a world-class public service well-equipped to serve Canada and Canadians now and into the future.

Technology is changing quickly and we are keeping pace. We are already working on the successor to the GCTools, which will have improved performance, flexibility and integration. This will become the foundation of a digital workspace for public servants and beyond.

To use our development resources wisely, we will focus our efforts on the upcoming suite of products. Legacy tools will go into “maintenance mode” and no new enhancements will be added.

Current products

GCcollab

A collaboration platform for public servants and Canadians. Sign up For GCcollab here.

GCintranet

The Government of Canada’s collection of employee resources. Visit GCintranet.

Legacy products

GCconnex

A legacy collaboration platform for public servants. Now in maintenance mode.

GCpedia

A legacy wiki-based collaborative workspace. Now in maintenance mode.

GCdirectory

A legacy Government of Canada employee directory accessible to federal public servants. Now in maintenance mode. Replacement product in development.

What’s in the pipeline

We are currently developing the core applications for the new digital workspace. So far we have launched GCcollab, GCwiki and are piloting GCmessage, all of which can be accessed using your GCcollab login.

Next up is work on the following three features:

Single sign-on

A single account that allows users to sign on to GCcollab and access all the products.

Career Marketplace

A refreshed product to connect people to opportunities, including jobs and mentorship.

Profile

A single user profile controlled by the user, available across all services and approved applications.

Collaboration is at the centre of digital enablement.
